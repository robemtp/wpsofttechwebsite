FAQ

Common Questions

Is there a free trial? ▼
Yes — every plan comes with a 30-day free trial. No credit card required. You get full access to all features in your chosen plan.
Can I add users mid-month? ▼
Absolutely. Add users anytime — you'll be billed pro-rata. Removing users is just as easy. No contracts, no hassle.
Are forms and workflows customizable? ▼
Yes — this is a core differentiator. Every form, checklist, workflow, and approval chain is fully customizable. You're never locked into templates.
How does the AI work? ▼
We use Claude, Anthropic's enterprise AI, connected directly to your safety data. It analyzes incidents, suggests corrective actions, generates reports, and answers questions in plain language.
Is my data secure? ▼
Your data is hosted on Microsoft Azure with enterprise-grade security. Encryption at rest and in transit, role-based access controls, and regular audits. Your data is always yours.
Can I switch plans later? ▼
Yes. Upgrade takes effect immediately. Downgrade takes effect at your next billing cycle. No penalties.
Do you offer implementation support? ▼
All plans include onboarding docs and video guides. Professional and Enterprise include live sessions. Enterprise includes a dedicated account manager.
